Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 30/09/2025

Last Updated: 30/09/2025

Colo Shot ("we", "our", "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information directly through the App.

However, the App uses third-party services that may collect information automatically for the purpose of delivering ads or improving their services.

2. Third-Party Services

The App integrates Google AdMob to display advertising, including rewarded ads. Google AdMob may collect and process information such as:

  • Device information (e.g., device identifiers, advertising ID, operating system)
  • Approximate location (if available)
  • Interaction with ads (e.g., impressions, clicks, rewards earned)
  • Data used for personalized advertising, if consent is granted

For more details, please review Google’s Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Consent: For personalized advertising and data collection in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and Switzerland, user consent is obtained through Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP).
  • Legitimate Interest / Non-Personalized Ads: Where consent is not provided, only non-personalized ads (which rely on contextual information, not user profiles) may be shown.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not directed toward children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided data via the App, please contact us and we will take immediate action.

5. Your Rights (EEA, UK, Switzerland)

If you are located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data processed by third parties like Google
  • Withdraw consent for personalized ads at any time via the App’s consent dialog
  • Lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority

6. Data Retention

We do not store any personal data. Data collected by third-party providers (such as Google) is retained according to their privacy policies.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
